About

I am a computer scientist and have been engineering software and systems since 2014. Areas where I have worked in include telecommunications, decentralized finance and freelance IT services. I hold a B.Sc. in computer science from the University of Iceland and studied information security and digital forensics at the graduate level at NTNU in Norway. I like empowering everyday users, power users and professionals in the field of information technology, security and privacy. Free and open source software and Unix-like operating systems are my specialty.

Aside from IT, I was born and raised in Iceland. Now I am based in Europe and the Americas and being a fan of my partner, plants and fruit trees, have grown particularly fond of South America and have lived there for about six years. I do enjoy fleshing out my Spanish vocabulary when I can, sometimes with online RPGs in Spanish for the sake of nostalgia on the rare occasions that I can.
